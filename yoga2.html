Asanas N Reps was born from a simple belief — that movement is medicine, and every person deserves a welcoming place to explore it.

Our all-new studio in Sector 16A, Faridabad, is thoughtfully crafted — warm lighting, spacious mats, and an atmosphere that lets you leave the world outside and simply breathe.
